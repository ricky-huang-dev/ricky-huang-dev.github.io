What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the core learning?

1. Through practice and practice, I am able to learn new skills such as coding.

2. Even though the reflections are my least favourite task, I know self reflection. is an important part of the learning process.

3. I need to set time limits for myself, otherwise I might just be wasting time instead of learning.

What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

Understanding values, empathy and self awareness is important for a team to work together. It would help you understand each other more, and know what to expect from your peers.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

I was surprised as to just how important the mindset of programmer is. For example, as a programmer you will need to be able to adapt quickly as there are almost infinite amount of things to learn in a programming language.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

For me the most challenging parts of core learning was writing the reflections and the blog. My writing skills is weak, so the activities involving writing I tend to put on the back burner.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development Bootcamp course?

Knowing your own strengths and weaknesses may help you in the course of learning.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practise programming instead? Justify your answer.

Personally I wouldn't say I enjoyed the core learning much, but they weren't a waste of time. They helped me understand who I am, taught me the mindset of a programmer, and somewhat improve my writing skills. Although I do feel the core learning aspect of the foundation could be reduced for more coding practice.
